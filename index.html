The dataset

The dataset for this project was taken from Opta which records the premier league stats for each of the team throughout the season
But this did not contain any geospatial elemnts so I had to manually add them in the dataset for the 20 teams


Interactive ScatterPlot

One of the domain questions were based on the players contributions to the team. This is a scatter chart for top 20 players with respect to the the following 3 attributes of goals scored, assists, and penalty kicks.
This can give us a quick way of seeing the distribution of players in the goal contributions, and we can also make out how many players are in the top 20 penalty kick takers that are already there in top goal scorers and assisters.
